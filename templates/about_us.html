About Us

Welcome To Mohmand Marble Zone


All Marble is a direct importer, Slabs, Marble, and products for bathrooms, kitchens, etc... All Marble is a Khyber Pakhtunkhuwa based company with a Warehouse and showroom located in Peshawar, Shabqadar Mohmand. 

Creating a bathroom that is aesthetically pleasing is all related to the finish of the marble you choose to install. As a company we pride ourselves as one of the few bathroom Marble companies in New Jersey offering the best flooring solutions for all your home renovation projects. We offer a wide range of great quality bathroom decor to fit all of your personal tastes and preferences. One unique quality you will identify with us is the ability to offer a wide variety of high quality material that you can't find anywhere else. 

Our wide marble collection is a great choice in creating a beautiful serene looking space. Marble is a wonderful option that we recommend to our clients as marble flawlessly blends with your floor creating an attractive high end appearance. 

Besides marble we also offer a variety of options such as floor Marble, borders, flowers tiles, porcelain, and mosaics. We leave it to the customer to choose the material they prefer and we are also happy to help and assess your home and give a couple of tips on which floor Marble will suit it best. 

 

Our Travertine tiles are a perfect choice for those who want to create a natural look in their bathroom. Travertine does not glitter like marble which makes it ideal when you want a toned down and neat look. 

Porcelain tiles have been around for a while and as a bathroom tile company, we believe we have some of the best porcelain tile options in Shabqadar. We provide tough and natural looking tiles that will neatly carpet every square inch of the bathing area. If you want something different from porcelain, you can never go wrong with glass tiles; especially when looking for a unique look for your bathing area. 

We can deliver the tiles right to your doorstep if you are a resident of Shabqadar. Once you make your purchases, we will organize the best transport for your tiles and ship them out in 1-3 days.
